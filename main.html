Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for implanting an idea into a target's subconscious.
